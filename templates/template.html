Analysis details

Understand the details behind the analysis.

Definitions


Messages: each individual block of text sent in Messenger (i.e. when you hit enter/send). This is loosely defined and may contain other elements e.g. audio calls.
Words: text or emojis that are separated by a space. These do not need to be actual words.

Note


All line graphs use a rolling {{ report_details.ma_days }} day average to smooth out the trends. This can be adjusted in config.py.
You can also adjust the date range for the analysis. For example, you can focus on a particular year. Also in config.py.
